Abstract

ğŸ§  LeVo 2 adopts a novel Hybrid LLM-Diffusion Architecture. The Language Model functions as the "Composer Brain," orchestrating the musical structure and performance nuances (solving how to sing and play), while the Diffusion Model acts as the "High-Fidelity Renderer," synthesizing intricate acoustic details. We designed a unique hierarchical structure to balance musicality with audio quality. Crucially, we utilize Multi-Stage Progressive Post-Training to explicitly inject music aesthetics priors into the LLM, drastically enhancing the model's controllability and ensuring the generation of structurally coherent, melodious music.
